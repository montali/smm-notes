\begin{example}
    Let $f(x) = \sin{(x)}$ and $x(t) = t^2$. We have $f(t) = \sin{t^2}$. To compute the derivative of $f$ with respect to $t$ we apply the chain rule:
    $$ \frac{df}{dt} = \frac{df}{dx} \cdot \frac{dx}{dt} = \left.\cos{(x)}\right|_{x = t^2} \cdot 2t = 2t\cos{(t^2)} $$
\end{example}
